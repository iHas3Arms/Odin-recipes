Simple Garlic Bread

This garlic bread is not only incredibly easy to make, but it tastes fabulous and is a great starter to hold you over until your mains arrive.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 80g softened butter
  • 3 cloves fresh chopped garlic
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Make the garlic butter by adding your butter, garlic, parsley and salt into a bowl.
  2. Mix it well and spread it on your bread.
  3. Cook in oven until it goes brown, golden and crispy.
  4. Enjoy!
