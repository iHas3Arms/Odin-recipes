High Protein Chicken Wrap

A chicken wrap can be great for those trying to watch your weight as you can throw almost whatever you want in it - lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber - to fill up your belly as opposed to unhealthy fats like milk chocolate. It's also a great option for those trying to pack on mass in the gym because chicken is a very lean protein, making it an undeniable neccessity in any gymgoer's diet.

Ingredients

  • 180g Chicken Breast
  • 1 Tortilla Wrap
  • 30g Shredded Light Cheddar/Mozzarella
  • Nando's Perinaise Mayo Sauce
  • Assortment of fillers, e.g. lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber etc.

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breast with your choice of seasonings and rub it in until it is fully covering the chicken.
  2. Cook the chicken in the oven at 180°C Fan/200°C Oven/Gas Mark 6 for 15-20 minutes until brown.
  3. Add your vegetable/fruit fillers on top of the wrap.
  4. Cut the chicken into cubes and lie them on the fillers.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on the wrap and top it all off with some perinaise sauce.
  6. Enjoy!
